The City’s FREE Financial Empowerment Centers can help you take control of your debt, deal with debt collectors, improve your credit, create a budget, open a bank account, start an emergency fund, save and plan for your future, and much more.

Call 311 to schedule a free one-on-one financial counseling appointment at a Financial Empowerment Center. All sites are listed below.  

  

  

The Financial Education Network (FEN) Directory provides access to free or low-cost financial education classes, workshops, hotlines, and one-on-one counseling services in New York City. This easy-to-search online directory of nonprofit organizations is your one-stop shop to help you take charge of your finances. Whether your financial goal is homeownership, starting and growing a small business, avoiding foreclosure, or investing in stocks or mutual funds, the FEN Directory has a service for you! 

Please contact providers in advance to confirm program schedules and details.
