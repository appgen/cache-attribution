Community Health Care Centers provide comprehensive and cost effective primary care and supportive services that promote access to health care. These centers accept health insurance and also provide services to the uninsured on a sliding fee scale.

IMPORTANT: Clinics vary in range of services provided. Please contact the clinic(s) in which you are interested to verify the type of services offered as well as location and hours of operation. For an updated list of community health centers, please visit http://www.chcanys.org
