For Residents: Cable & Open Video Requests

For requests or concerns regarding your cable service, you may contact your service provider's customer service office using the contact information below.

If your situation isn't resolved by the service provider, you have several additional options:

  • Submit a Cable Television and OVS System Customer Service Request online.
  • Go to 311 Online at nyc.gov/311 or text 311NYC (311692).
  • Write DoITT or the New York State Public Service Commission:
