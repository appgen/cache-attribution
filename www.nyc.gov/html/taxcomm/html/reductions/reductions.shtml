Actions on Applications in 2012 Reducing Assessments or Reclassifying Property

Listed here are Tax Commission actions during 2012 reducing assessments or reclassifying property.

KEY: YR=Assessment year (11=2011/12; 12=2012/13);
R=Reduction implemented by remission of taxes;
B=Borough (1=Manhattan, 2=Bronx, 3=Brooklyn, 4=Queens, 5=Staten Island); TC=Tax Class or subclass.

Classification claims. Reductions are expressed in total actual assessed value. For condominiums, actions shown are for representative lots only.

The Tax Commission actions during 2012 available here are in .PDF format. To download and print you will need the latest copy of the Adobe Acrobat Reader.
